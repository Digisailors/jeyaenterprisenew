R&D

Jeya Enterprises R&D effort spans mineral discovery, environment protection, mineral processing, product and application development. Expert teams develop, refine and evolve the technical means to transform mineral rock into end-user application material. To develop consistent high-quality customized grades, Jeya Enterprises scientists conduct sophisticated research in mineral grinding, beneficiation and surface modification techniques. A unique mineral sorting & grinding system is the back bone to new product development.

Quality Assurance

Due to Jeya Enterprises unique quality assurance system each finished product batch can be traced right back to the mining face from which it was extracted. Stringent grading and sorting of mined ore from extraction to processing ensures quality consistency.

We are one of the leading Manufacturers & Exporters of lime & limestone powder.
