R&D

Jeya Enterprises R&D effort spans mineral discovery, environment protection, mineral processing, product and application development. Expert teams develop, refine and evolve the technical means to transform mineral rock into end-user application material.
To develop consistent high-quality customized grades, Jeya Enterprises scientists conduct sophisticated research in mineral grinding, beneficiation and surface modification techniques. A unique mineral sorting & grinding system is the back bone to new product development.
