Who We Are

Our every action is directed towards producing and processing the earth’s minerals through the most economic and efficient approach so as to provide the best materials that lead to fulfilling consumer’s needs and improving ecologically healthy environment. Supplying, Importing and Exporting of highly acclaimed variety of Minerals.

Our Security

For the assurance of the quality of the minerals we conduct various stringent tests under the strict supervision of our quality control executives. The series of tests are performed at various levels so as to ensure the quality of the products delivered to the customers.

Our Process

We offer product like Dolomite,Calcite powder. These products are processed at our highly sophisticated facility using quality-certified grades of constituents, which is sourced from reliable merchandisers.
